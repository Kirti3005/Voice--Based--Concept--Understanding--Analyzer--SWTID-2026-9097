Data science is an interdisciplinary field that uses scientific methods, algorithms, processes, and systems to extract knowledge and insights from structured and unstructured data. It combines statistics, mathematics, programming, domain expertise, and machine learning. Key concepts include data collection, data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), feature engineering, statistical modeling, hypothesis testing, data visualization, predictive analytics, and big data. Tools include Python, R, SQL, Jupyter notebooks, pandas, NumPy, matplotlib, scikit-learn, and Spark. The data science workflow includes problem definition, data acquisition, preprocessing, modeling, evaluation, and deployment. Applications include business intelligence, healthcare analytics, and financial forecasting.
